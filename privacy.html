Data Processing Overview

SSL / TLS Encryption

For security reasons and to protect the transmission of confidential content, this site uses SSL/TLS encryption. You can recognize this by the "https://" in your address bar. No personal data is collected through this mechanism.

Server Log Files

Our hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in server log files, which your browser automatically transmits. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, referrer URL, and the date/time of the server request.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in the technical security of the website).

Retention period: Log files are deleted automatically after 7 days.

Contact Form

When you reach out via our contact form, we store the information you provide (name, email address, and message content) solely to process your request. We do not share this data with third parties without your consent.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (processing necessary to respond to your request) and Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in customer communication).

Retention period: Contact data is deleted once your request has been fully handled and no further communication is necessary, unless statutory retention obligations apply (up to 7 years for business correspondence under Austrian tax law).

Purchase of Steam Keys

When you purchase a Steam Key via our webshop, we process your name and email address to fulfill your order and deliver the key. Payment processing is handled by a third-party payment provider; we do not store full payment details.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (performance of a contract).

Retention period: Order data is retained for 7 years as required by Austrian accounting and tax law (BAO), then deleted.

Cookies

To facilitate the purchase of Steam Keys, we use a technically necessary session cookie. This identifies your shopping cart session and is deleted once you close your browser. No tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies are used on this website.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in operating a functional webshop); no consent required for strictly necessary cookies.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR): You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): You may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): You may request deletion of your data, unless retention is legally required.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR).
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR).
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR): You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: cosmicdev@outlook.com

We will try our best to respond to your request within 30 days as required by law.
