Privacy Policy

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. We process your data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Austrian Data Protection Act (DSG).

Data Processing Overview

SSL or TLS Encryption

For security reasons and to protect the transmission of confidential content—such as the inquiries you send to us via the contact form—this site uses SSL/TLS encryption. You can recognize this by the "https://" in your address bar.

Server Log Files

Our hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in "server log files," which your browser automatically transmits to us. This includes your IP address, browser type, and the time of the server request. This is necessary for the technical security of the website.

Contact Form & Steam Keys

When you reach out via our contact form or purchase a Steam Key, we store the information you provide (name/email) solely to process your request or fulfill your order. We do not share this data without your consent.

Cookies

To facilitate the purchase of Steam Keys, we use a necessary "Session Cookie." This identifies your shopping cart and is deleted once you close your browser. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Your Rights & Authority

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data at any time. If you believe your data rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Österreichische Datenschutzbehörde).

Note: Processing of data requests may take up to 30 days as per legal guidelines, though we strive to handle them as quickly as possible.
